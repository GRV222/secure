Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of specific data, or all of your data, without deleting your entire account.

Delete specific content in the app

  • Posts: open any of your posts and choose Delete.
  • Profile details: edit or clear them in Settings → Edit Profile.
  • Contacts: remove any contact from your Contacts list.

Request a full data deletion by email

To request deletion of your personal data, email gauravbathiya21@gmail.com from your account email address with the subject "Delete my data". Tell us whether you want all data removed or only specific items. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email.

What data we can delete

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone, bio).
  • Your posts, images, audio, and video.
  • Your comments, ratings, and messages (deleted or anonymized).
  • Your contacts and relationship labels.

What may be retained

We may retain limited records only where required by law, or to prevent fraud and abuse, for the minimum time necessary. Anonymous, aggregated analytics that cannot identify you may be retained.
