2. WHAT WE COLLECT

Information you provide:

  • Name and display name

  • Age

  • Posts, photos, videos, audio, text

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show your posts to the community
  • To calculate ratings and rankings

  • To distribute competition rewards
  • To prevent abuse and fake accounts

We NEVER sell your data. We NEVER share your selfie publicly. We NEVER use your data for advertising. SECURE products are ad-free.

4. YOUR SELFIE

Your selfie is used ONLY to verify you are a real person. It is:

  • Never shown publicly
  • Never shared with anyone
  • Stored securely and encrypted
  • Used only for identity verification

5. DATA STORAGE

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with servers in Asia. We use industry-standard encryption for all data in transit and at rest.

6. TOKEN ECONOMY

SHREE, DA, and SHREEDA tokens are platform credits only. They are:

SECURE requires users to be 13 years or older. Users under 18 must have parental or guardian consent.

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we delete it immediately.
