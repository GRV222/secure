Content Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

SECURE is a platform for real creators. We believe in creative freedom within a safe and respectful community.

✅ WHAT IS ALLOWED

  • Original artwork, paintings, sketches
  • Music, classical and contemporary
  • Poetry, prose, creative writing
  • Photography (artistic)
  • Dance performances
  • Short films and videos
  • Code art and digital creations
  • Cultural and traditional art forms
  • Educational content about your craft
  • Work in progress and journey posts

❌ WHAT IS NOT ALLOWED

  • Nudity or sexually explicit content
  • Violence or graphic imagery
  • Hate speech or discrimination
  • Harassment of any user
  • Spam or repetitive content
  • Misleading or false information
  • Copyright infringement
  • Political propaganda
  • Promotion of illegal activities
  • Content targeting minors inappropriately

LIVE STREAMING POLICY

Live streams must follow all content rules above. Additional rules:

  • No nudity or sexual content ever
  • No harassment of viewers
  • No promotion of harmful products

Violations result in immediate stream termination and account review.

REPORTING CONTENT

See something that violates these rules? Use the report button on any post or contact us at secure@bathiyagroup.com

We review all reports within 48 hours.

CONSEQUENCES

  • First violation — warning
  • Second violation — temporary ban
  • Third violation — permanent ban
  • Severe violations — immediate permanent ban
