SECURE — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

SECURE ("we", "our", "the app") is a merit-based creative platform where creators are seen for their work through honest human ratings. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: your name, email address, and (optionally) phone number, used to create and secure your account.
  • Content you create: posts, images, audio, video, comments, ratings, and profile details you choose to share.
  • Usage data: anonymous app-activity events (e.g. posts created, ratings given) used to improve the app. This contains no message content or personal identifiers beyond your account ID.
  • Device data: basic technical information and crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics) to keep the app stable.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate core features: posting, rating, competitions, contacts, and your wallet.
  • To keep your account and the community safe.
  • To improve the app through anonymous, aggregated analytics.
  • To send you notifications you have not turned off.

3. How Your Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal data. Content you choose to publish (posts, profile, ratings) is visible to other users by design. Private relationship labels on contacts are visible only to you and the connected person. We use trusted service providers (Google Firebase) to host data securely.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase with industry-standard protections, including server-side access rules and app-integrity verification.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & edit: you can view and edit your profile in the app.
  • Delete your account: see our Account Deletion page.
  • Delete specific data: see our Data Deletion page.
  • Notifications: manage or turn off push notifications in Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

SECURE is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "last updated" date.
