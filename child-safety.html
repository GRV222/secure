Our Zero-Tolerance Policy

SECURE maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form. This applies to all content, interactions, and user behaviour on our platform.

The safety and protection of minors is our highest priority. No exceptions.

Prohibited Content and Behaviour

The following are strictly prohibited on SECURE and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to relevant authorities:

  • Any content that sexually exploits, abuses, or endangers minors
  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) in any format — images, video, audio, or text
  • Grooming behaviour — adults attempting to establish inappropriate relationships with minors
  • Solicitation of minors for sexual purposes
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting CSAE material
  • Content that sexualises minors in any way
  • Using the platform to facilitate contact with minors for exploitative purposes
Identity Verification

Real People Only

Every SECURE account requires a private selfie during registration. This is stored securely and never displayed publicly. It confirms that a real adult human is behind each account, reducing the risk of fake or exploitative profiles targeting minors.

Age Requirements

SECURE is intended for users aged 13 and over. Users under 13 are not permitted to create accounts. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's app usage.

Detection and Enforcement

SECURE employs the following measures to detect and prevent CSAE:

  • Manual review of reported content within 24 hours
  • User reporting tools available on every post and profile
  • Immediate content removal upon confirmed violation
  • Permanent account ban for any CSAE-related violation
  • Cooperation with law enforcement and relevant authorities
  • Reporting confirmed CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and local Indian authorities
Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report any content or behaviour they believe violates these standards through multiple channels:

  • In-app report button on every post, comment, and profile
  • Direct email to our safety team (see contact below)
  • All reports are reviewed within 24 hours
  • Reporters remain anonymous
  • No retaliation against reporters
Content Moderation

Community Standards

All user-generated content on SECURE — including posts, discussions, and profile information — must comply with our Community Standards. Content that violates child safety policies is removed immediately upon detection or report.

Discussions (Messaging)

Our Discussions feature is monitored for inappropriate contact. Users can block and report any other user at any time. Unsolicited contact with minors is treated as a serious violation.
